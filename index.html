About Us

Mission: To nurture and develop young chess talents through expert coaching, community engagement, and promoting chess as a tool for education and character building in schools and homes.

Vision: To be the leading chess academy in Kenya fostering future champions, promoting discipline and strategic thinking through inclusive and accessible chess programs for all children.

Coach James Muchiri

Founder of Tumaini Chess Academy, experienced player and certified FIDE Arbiter. Coach James is passionate about developing future chess champions through inspiration and expert guidance.

Programs & Services

We offer a variety of chess programs including:

  • Home Coaching: Personalized chess coaching sessions conducted at your home for effective learning.
  • Chess in Schools: Integration of chess training in school programs to foster young talents.
  • Holiday Chess Camps & Weekend Group Sessions: Engaging chess camps and group activities during school holidays and weekends suitable for all junior levels.
